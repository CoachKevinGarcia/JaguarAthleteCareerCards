Coaching Notes
Four Straight Years of Improvement
Mario improved in both shot put and discus every single season from 9th through 12th grade without exception. Discus: 60'6" → 91'7" → 107'0" → 121'11". Shot Put: 24'8" → 31'4" → 35'6" → 38'9". That kind of unbroken upward arc over four years reflects a student of the event who was consistently getting stronger and developing technically.
Discus is the Standout
His 121'11" discus puts him 5th in the River Valley League and top 300 in Southern California — a legitimately strong result for a program without a dedicated throws specialist coaching staff. He hit 121+ feet in four consecutive meet appearances down the stretch. That consistency at a high mark, not just a single peak, is the sign of a developed thrower.
League Finals Heartbreak
His season ended with a foul series at River Valley League Championships in the discus — potentially costing him a league title in an event where he was ranked 5th in the entire league. He went 121'9" just before at the Patriot dual. Fouling out at League when in the best shape of the season is one of the harder endings a throws athlete can face.
