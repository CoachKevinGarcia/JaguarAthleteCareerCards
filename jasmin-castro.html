Coaching Notes
Three-Year Progression
Castro improved her discus season best every year: 55'4" → 63'2" → 73'6". That's 18'2" of total growth over three seasons, with the biggest single-year jump coming in her senior year — 10'4" from her 2025 SB to her 2026 PR. Late-bloomer pattern: she fouled at the 2025 RVL Championships, then came back and posted a massive PR at the 2026 RVL Championships. Peaked at exactly the right time.
Championships Performance
The 73'6" at RVL Championships was 5'10" farther than her previous best of 67'6". Championship-day PRs of that magnitude indicate she had more in the tank all season. Her competition-day ceiling appears higher than her practice ceiling — that's a mental and physical strength worth noting.
Long Jump — Secondary Event
Competed in the long jump in 10th and 11th grade, posting a 6'2.5" PR in 2025. Did not compete in LJ as a senior — focus shifted entirely to discus. Given the big throw numbers, the right call. If she picks up any field event in college, the runway and explosiveness that drives discus is transferable.
