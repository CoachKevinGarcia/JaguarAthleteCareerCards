3 major injuries · 4 seasons · never quit
10TH GRADE — BROKEN WRIST
Fractured his wrist in the pre-season of his sophomore year. Missed the majority of the season with DNS marks across 200m, 400m, and 800m. Competed sparingly and fought through the pain.
⚠️ Most of sophomore season lost to injury
10TH GRADE — REFUSED TO QUIT
Despite the injury, came back to post PRs in 110mH (17.09) and 300mH (42.63). Earned CIF-SS D3 berths in both hurdles events. Won the Mountain Valley League Championship in 300m Hurdles.
🏆 MVL 300mH Champion · CIF Qualifier · PRs in both hurdles
11TH GRADE — HAMSTRING + BROKEN ELBOW
Opened junior year with a nagging hamstring injury, missing the first 3 weeks of the season. Returned at the Raincross Invitational — and in his very first high heat of 110m Hurdles, fell over a hurdle and broke his elbow. His entire junior season was over in a single race.
⚠️ Season ended at Raincross — 1 week back from hamstring
12TH GRADE — THE RETURN
Returns to Raincross as a senior — healthy, focused, and dominant. Wins the 110m Hurdles Raincross Traditional Riverside City Championship in commanding fashion, finishing nearly a full second ahead of the runner-up. Goes on to set PRs in every single event he enters his senior year.
🏆 Raincross City Champion · PRs across 110mH, 300mH, HJ, 100m, 200m, 400m

Coaching Notes & Trajectory
110m Hurdles
Year-to-year peaks: 18.33 → 17.09 → (injury) → 16.47. Senior season shows 4 consecutive sub-17 performances including 4 wins. All 4 senior races ended 1st place. The 39.77 in 300mH puts him at Top 56 in California — his most nationally competitive event.
High Jump
Went from 5'4" SB as a freshman to 6'0" School Record as a senior. NH results mixed in suggest technical inconsistency still being refined. 6'0" is River Valley League #1 and Top 135 in California. Ceiling appears higher with more focused training.
Sprint Development
100m PR improved from 12.96 (9th) → 12.57 (10th) → 11.77 (12th) — a full 1.2 second drop over career. Added 200m and 400m as senior events, PRing in both. The sprint base is clearly elevating the hurdles pop-off.
Resilience Profile
Broken wrist (10th), hamstring (11th), broken elbow (11th) — three significant injuries across two seasons. Posted PRs even during the broken wrist season. Returned senior year completely injury-free with PRs in every event entered. Mental toughness is a defining characteristic.
