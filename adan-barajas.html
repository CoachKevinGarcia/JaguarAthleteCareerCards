Coaching Notes
Development Arc
Spent 11th grade on JV developing fundamentals in the 100 and 200. Made the transition to varsity competition by senior year and PRed in every individual event. The jump from 12.63 to 12.38 in the 100m and 27.04c to 26.74 in the 200m shows real growth between seasons.
Relay Contributor
Became a reliable relay piece in his senior year, contributing to 4×100, 4×200, 4×400, and SMR squads. The team's 4×100 improved significantly with his involvement on the varsity roster. His sprint speed makes him a useful leg in multiple relay configurations.
Senior Season Takeaway
PRed in 100m, 200m, and 400m — all three individual events — in his final season. The 12.38 100m at Corona Relays was his career highlight. With more reps in the 400m, there's likely more room to drop time. Finishes his Jaguar career as a versatile sprint contributor.
