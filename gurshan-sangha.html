Coaching Notes
Early Development
Two seasons in, still developing. His PR of 16.48 at the Patriot dual meet was his cleanest FAT result — the 16.64c in 9th grade was hand-timed and slightly faster on the clock, but the trend is in the right direction. He showed up every dual meet and competed, which matters at this stage.
Consistency Note
Times varied significantly meet to meet — from 16.48 to 17.97 in the 100m in the same season. That spread indicates the mechanics aren't fully grooved yet. More consistent acceleration and drive phase development would tighten that range considerably in a 3rd season.
Outlook
With 2 full years of eligibility remaining (11th and 12th grade), Gurshan is still early in his athletic career. The foundation is there — he has competed consistently and continues to improve. Sprint mechanics work and dedicated weekly training could realistically put him into the 15-second range by junior year.
