Coaching Notes
Multi-Event Presence
Giovanni entered the throwing circle for the first time (on record) as a senior and went 33'8.5" in shot put and 82'4" in discus — enough to rank in the top 30 and top 40 in the River Valley League respectively. That's not a trivial result for an athlete with no prior posted marks in these events.
Consistent Dual-Meet Performer
Won or placed first in the throwing events in multiple dual meets. JV-flight competition exposes inconsistencies quickly — he held up well across the season, staying competitive in throws from March through April. The 200m was a one-meet sample but his 26.85 shows real sprint ability alongside the throws.
Late Start, Real Upside
With only one documented season, there's no pre-senior baseline to compare. Arriving with both throwing and sprinting ability as a senior suggests natural athleticism. If he had started in the throwing events in 9th or 10th grade, the development arc would likely have taken him further. Finishes as a multi-event contributor in his only Jaguar season.
