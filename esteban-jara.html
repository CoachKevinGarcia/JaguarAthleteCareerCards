Coaching Notes
Distance Foundation
Four years of distance running built an aerobic base that paid dividends in his senior relay legs. His 2:29 800m in 11th grade is his strongest mark from a purely competitive standpoint in distance — solid for a varsity JV athlete. The 14:14 3200m junior year showed real endurance development year over year.
Senior Year Sprint Crossover
Switched to the sprint events as a senior and immediately contributed on the relay squad. Running sub-13 in the 100 and sub-27 in the 200 in his first full season of sprints — having never run those events before — is a reflection of his base fitness. His aerobic conditioning likely helped him stay strong in the back half of relay 400m legs.
Program Versatility
Esteban's willingness to move across the event groups as a senior shows what kind of teammate he was. The program needed relay depth and he stepped into that role without complaint. His 4×400 legs in the 4:06 race and 4×100 at 48-second pace showed genuine sprint speed for a distance runner making the transition mid-career.
