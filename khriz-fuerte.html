Coaching Notes
Distance Background (Shafter HS, 9th–11th)
Competed at Shafter HS for three years as a middle-distance runner. His best marks came in 10th grade — 3:14 800m and 7:44 1600m. The following year he transitioned to sprint events at Shafter, running the 100, 200, and 400. His 1:17 400m in 11th grade connects the two phases of his career.
Transfer Senior Year — Jurupa Valley
Transferred to Jurupa Valley for his senior season and competed in JV-flight sprint events, the 800m, and even attempted pole vault. The transition to a new program mid-career limits the amount of data on record here. He contributed where he could in a short window at the end of his high school career.
Four Years of Effort
Four years across two schools and five different events tells a story of an athlete who kept showing up. He tried distance, transitioned to sprints, attempted a technical field event, and finished his career as a Jaguar. The competitive experience accumulated from 9th through 12th grade matters even when individual marks are modest.
