Long Jump Progression
Improved her LJ season best from 9'7.5" (11th) to 9'10" (12th) — a modest but consistent gain. She won three dual meet long jump events in her senior year, showing she could compete and place in the field. The foul series at the RVL Championships was a costly end to the season; making a legal mark under meet pressure is a technical area that needed more competition reps.
Triple Jump
Her one recorded triple jump mark (19'5" in 10th grade) didn't reappear in her junior or senior seasons. It's unclear whether she stopped training the event or just didn't compete in it again. That mark in 10th grade is worth noting — with two more years of development it may have opened a different door.
Consistency Challenge
Fouls and DNS results appear across all three seasons. At the RVL Championships she went foul series — missing a qualifying mark on her biggest stage. Technical consistency in the approach and takeoff needs to be a priority. The jump itself (9'10" PR) shows she has the athleticism; controlling it under pressure is the gap.
