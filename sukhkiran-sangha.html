Her 2026 season was her best 800m campaign, with seven races and a PR at vs Patriot in April. Running a consistent 3:03–3:04 range at the end of the season shows she found her level and can hold it.

Her career-best 400m (1:16.88) actually came as a junior, not a senior. In 2025 she ran the 400m regularly and consistently — six races with five under 1:20. As a senior she shifted primary focus to the 800m, which makes sense given where she's strongest, but the 400m speed is still in her base.
Relay Contributor
Three seasons of relay appearances. Was part of the 4×400 squad each year, including a first-place leg at La Sierra in 2026 and a 5:04 split in 2025 Senior Night. The relay work supplemented her individual events throughout her career and shows she's coachable as a team piece.
Versatility
Sukhkiran also appeared in the 100m (PR 15.90), 200m (PR 34.94c), 4×200, and SMR events across her career — and even attempted pole vault in 2026. That range shows she was willing to do what the team needed. The 15.90 100m as a junior is legitimately quick and speaks to her raw speed even as she specialized toward distance.
Sprint Speed Note

Her hand-timed marks in JV competition (2024) are plentiful and mostly consistent in the 1:20–1:22 range before she broke through with auto-timing in varsity competition.
